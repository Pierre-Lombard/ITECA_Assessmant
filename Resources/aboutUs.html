Our story

Soil-to-Soil is a small business based in Pretoria. They make their existence by selling seedlings to the community. With the lockdown, they have lost most of their clients, as foot traffic in the shopping center has reduced. Even though the lockdown restrictions have eased, the business has not recovered. Thus, they are ready to move to an online-based business structure. This will allow them to close their brick and mortar store and do everything from their farm. This will also free up the budget to allow for expansions within the farm.
